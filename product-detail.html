Premium Egyptian Onions

Available for Export

Our premium Egyptian onions are known for their exceptional quality, distinctive flavor, and long shelf life. Grown in the fertile Nile Delta region, these onions benefit from the rich soil and ideal growing conditions that Egypt is famous for.

Available in various sizes and packaging options to meet your specific requirements, our onions are perfect for retail, wholesale, and food service industries.
