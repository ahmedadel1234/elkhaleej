Who We Are

Global Fresh Produce Experts

Elkhaleej for import & export is a global import-export company of fresh fruit and vegetables, an Egyptian Company. It also has offices in Senegal, Benin and Qatar. The firm has more than 2 years of experience in the fresh fruit and vegetables business.

Elkhaleej for import & export has a wide portfolio of over 25 produce items, but focuses on garlic, lemons, onion, potatoes, carrot and orange. The company works with more than 30 loyal and 200 customers in 25 countries.
